About the Shop

We specialize in component-level diagnostics and repair of vintage arcade hardware, pinball machines, and classic computing systems. No board too old, no fault too obscure.

Using professional diagnostic equipment including Fluke 9010/9100 troubleshooters, we trace faults down to the individual chip. From TTL logic to custom ASICs, electromechanical assemblies to CRT monitors—we've seen it all and fixed most of it.

Whether it's a temperamental JAMMA board, a silent pinball machine, or a vintage computer that won't POST, we'll diagnose the problem and get it running again.

Services

🕹️

Arcade Board Repair

JAMMA, MVS, CPS, and classic discrete boards. Component-level diagnostics, chip replacement, and full restoration. We handle everything from Pac-Man to Neo Geo.

🎯

Pinball Restoration

Solid-state and electromechanical pinball machines. Board repair, switch matrix troubleshooting, solenoid rebuilds, and playfield restoration.

💾

Vintage Computing

Classic 8-bit and 16-bit systems. Memory failures, video issues, power supply rebuilds, and peripheral restoration. From Apple II to ZX Spectrum.

📺

CRT Monitor Service

Arcade monitor repair and cap kits. Geometry adjustment, flyback replacement, and chassis rebuilds. Keep that original glow alive.

🔧

Custom Modifications

RGB mods, HDMI conversions, switcher installations, and custom controller interfaces. Modern conveniences for vintage hardware.

🔬

Diagnostic Services

Can't figure out what's wrong? We offer detailed diagnostic reports using professional test equipment. Know exactly what needs fixing.
